Palettica

What is Palettica?

Palettica is a powerful and intuitive tool designed to simplify your color palette creation process. Built with both designers and developers in mind, it offers a seamless and efficient user experience.

Download Palettica for your device

Download version 1.0 Open in Git Hub Apps

Key Features

  • 🔥 Mix any two colours, in selected ratio and immediately view the result.
  • 📲 Save your favorite colours with one button press. You can copy hex values and delete colours.
  • 🤙 Unmix mode - enter your hex value and find your colours for mixing together for your entered colour!
  • 🔥 Simple and intuitive design with user friendly interface
